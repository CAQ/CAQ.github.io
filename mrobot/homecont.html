What's MRobot?

  • MRobot is a robot, whose `foot' is a car and brain (and eyes, etc.) is a mobile phone.
  • The car is controlled by the program running in the phone, while the phone uses the camera to see the outside world, then decides where to go.

 

 

 

 

Why MRobot?

  • "A Robot in Every Home" by Bill Gates (Scientific American, January 2007 Issue)
  • Service Robot, Security Robot and so on are developing but still cost a lot.
  • So we need a cheap robot for popularization.
